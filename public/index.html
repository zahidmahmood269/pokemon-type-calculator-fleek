Pokemon Stat Calculator

Calculate Pokemon Stats

How to Use

  1. Enter the Pokemon's base stat for the specific stat you're calculating.
  2. Input the IV (Individual Value) for the stat (0-31).
  3. Enter the EV (Effort Value) invested in the stat (0-252).
  4. Specify the Pokemon's level (1-100).
  5. Click Calculate Stat to see the result.

Benefits

  • Accurately predict your Pokemon's stats at any level.
  • Optimize EV training by seeing the impact on stats.
  • Plan your team's stat distribution for competitive battles.
  • Understand how IVs and base stats affect overall performance.
  • Great tool for both casual players and competitive battlers.
